Procrastination is an action where a person delays or postpones a task or set of tasks until its deadline or in some cases never. Procrastination is considered the brain’s neurological response to stress. Which is the same fight, flight, or freeze reactions we all can all experience. Tackling procrastination can help an individual’s mental health. Most chronic procrastinators have mental health issues which can complicate life. Mitigating or stopping procrastinating could potentially even turn someone’s life around.
